 URBAN DIGKIT is part of the Blue City project {➔}, an Innosuisse funded research led by EPFL that explore the role of digital twins in urban context.

Bases on a research project conducted in Lugano about waste infrastructure, this platform documents protocol for collecting public online content.
The research explore how urban issues are perceive and lived by citizens though content their share on online platform like social media and review website.

This platform turns these methods into step-by-step procedure that can be adapted to different cities and topics. By making the research methods accessible and reproductible, it enables urban administration to use online content as a source of insight without previous technical experience.
